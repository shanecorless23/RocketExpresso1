Welcome To Rocket Expresso

A Restaurant that suits your healthy needs!

Welcome to Rocket Expresso, where we embark on an exciting mission to delight your taste buds with exquisite food and coffee. Whether you're a dedicated fitness enthusiast or a leisurely Sunday stroller seeking a delectable treat, we have something special in store for everyone.

For coffee aficionados craving that perfect cup, Rocket Expresso is your haven. We are thrilled to announce our presence in two convenient locations: one on the Ballinagh Road and another on Main Street in Cavan.

Come visit us at either of our locations and treat yourself to an unforgettable experience.

Services Provided

Food

At Rocket Expresso, we are passionate about serving delicious and wholesome food options that cater to your cravings. Our menu features a delectable selection of sandwiches and wraps, crafted with the finest ingredients to tantalize your taste buds.
